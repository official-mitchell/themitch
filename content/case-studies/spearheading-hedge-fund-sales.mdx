## Overview

This case study covers the development and execution of a go-to-market strategy targeting hedge funds and institutional traders as customers for Dexible.

## Challenge

Hedge funds represented a high-value segment but had unique operational requirements:

- Complex compliance and risk management needs
- Customized integration requirements
- High touch sales and support expectations
- Significant integration costs

## Solution

I developed a comprehensive hedge fund sales strategy that included:

- **Custom Integration Support**: Dedicated team for hedge fund onboarding
- **Operational Automation**: Tools to streamline their trading workflows
- **Compliance Framework**: Solutions addressing their regulatory requirements
- **Account Management**: High-touch support model for this segment

The automation tools integrated Dexible's API with hedge fund trading infrastructure, automating order routing and execution monitoring.

## Results

The hedge fund sales initiative delivered:

- **25% reduction in operational costs** through automation
- **3 major hedge fund clients** signed
- **Established market credibility** in institutional DeFi
- **Repeatable playbook** for future enterprise deals
- **New revenue stream** from professional trading segment
